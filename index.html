Sound familiar?

You’ve tried the blockers. The timers. The screen time reports that only made you feel worse.

You deleted them for the same reason: the real problem happens in a split second. The urge hits, you tap without thinking, and you’re already scrolling before you’ve decided what you actually want to do.

The phone isn’t the whole problem. The blank moment is. That tiny gap where you don’t know what to do next.

Most focus apps try to stop the scroll. Flowy tries to answer what comes next.

How it works
01

You reach for the scroll.

Out of habit. Not because you planned to. Flowy catches that moment before the drift starts.

02

Flowy offers a tiny trade.

Something small that fits the moment. You can do it, ask for another one, or skip it instantly.

03

Flowy adapts next time.

If a trade is too much, Flowy offers something lighter next time. If you keep accepting, it gradually gets more ambitious. No guilt. Just better suggestions over time.

Why it works

A trade, not a lock.

When you reach for the scroll, Flowy doesn’t trap you. It offers a small next move that’s easier to say yes to than starting from nothing.

The moment matters.

The real opportunity is the split second between impulse and scroll. Flowy uses that window to offer something better — and lets you say no instantly.

You stay in control.

Start the trade. Ask for a different task. Or skip it completely. Flowy never forces the choice. It just gets better at offering one you might actually take.

Behind Flowy

We built Flowy because we’re exactly the kind of people who delete focus apps. We’ve ignored screen time limits, tapped past reminders, and watched willpower fail in real time.

So we started building the tool we actually wanted: something that doesn’t fight the impulse to grab your phone, but gives you a better place to land when you do.

Flowy is being built by a small team across product, behavioral psychology, and design — with one goal: make the better choice easier in the moment it actually matters.

Flowy Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED: MARCH 2026

At Flowy, we believe in digital wellness and treating your data with respect. This simple Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information while you are on our waitlist.

1. What Information We Collect

When you join our waitlist, the only personal information we collect is your email address.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your email address for one specific purpose: to keep you in the loop. We will only use it to:

  • Notify you when Flowy officially launches.
  • Send you important updates regarding your early access status.

3. How We Protect and Store Your Data

We do not build custom databases for the waitlist. Your email is securely submitted and stored using Google Forms and Google Sheets. We rely on Google's enterprise-grade security to keep your information safe.

4. Third-Party Sharing (We don't sell your data)

We will never sell, rent, or trade your email address to advertisers or third parties. We are building an app to help you regain your focus, not to mine your data.

5. Your Rights

You own your data. If you change your mind and want to be removed from the waitlist, or if you want us to delete your email address from our records, simply email us at team@heyflowy.app with the subject "Remove from Waitlist," and we will delete your information immediately.

6. Future Updates

When the Flowy iOS app officially launches, we will release a comprehensive, app-specific Privacy Policy that outlines exactly how on-device data is handled (spoiler: your app usage data will stay on your device).

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy or how we handle your data, we're real people building this in Istanbul. Reach out to us at team@heyflowy.app.
