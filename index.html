Why it works

A trade, not a block.

When you reach for the scroll, Flowy doesn't say no. It offers something better — a real task you actually wanted to do. 10 minutes later, it's done.

Why the trade works.

A 2023 study published in PNAS (N=280) found that combining friction with a substitute behavior reduced app openings by 57%. Blocking alone didn't last. Replacing the habit did. That's the trade mechanic.

You're always in control.

Accept the trade, skip it, or take a break. Flowy never locks you in. No guilt. No punishment. Always your choice.

Flowy Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED: MARCH 2026

At Flowy, we believe in digital wellness and treating your data with respect. This simple Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information while you are on our waitlist.

1. What Information We Collect

When you join our waitlist, the only personal information we collect is your email address.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your email address for one specific purpose: to keep you in the loop. We will only use it to:

  • Notify you when Flowy officially launches.
  • Send you important updates regarding your early access status.

3. How We Protect and Store Your Data

We do not build custom databases for the waitlist. Your email is securely submitted and stored using Google Forms and Google Sheets. We rely on Google's enterprise-grade security to keep your information safe.

4. Third-Party Sharing (We don't sell your data)

We will never sell, rent, or trade your email address to advertisers or third parties. We are building an app to help you regain your focus, not to mine your data.

5. Your Rights

You own your data. If you change your mind and want to be removed from the waitlist, or if you want us to delete your email address from our records, simply email us at team@heyflowy.app with the subject "Remove from Waitlist," and we will delete your information immediately.

6. Future Updates

When the Flowy iOS app officially launches, we will release a comprehensive, app-specific Privacy Policy that outlines exactly how on-device data is handled (spoiler: your app usage data will stay on your device).

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy or how we handle your data, we're real people building this in Istanbul. Reach out to us at team@heyflowy.app.
